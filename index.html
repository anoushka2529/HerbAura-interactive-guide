Some frequently asked questions

Can I regrow herbs from kitchen scraps?

Yes! Herbs like coriander,spring onions,and basil can be regrown from stems or seeds found in your kitchen.

What causes leaves to become pale?

Pale leaves are often caused by nutrient deficiencies, improper watering, pests, or insufficient light. Environmental stress and poor soil drainage can also contribute to discoloration.

How do I care for herbs during extreme weather conditions?

Here are some seasonal care tips for protecting your plants from heatwaves, frost, or heavy rainfall. For instance:
Use mulch during summers to retain soil moisture.
Move potted herbs indoors during winter.

How do i choose a plant?

Choose a plant based on your available space, light conditions, and the level of care you're willing to provide. Consider factors like climate, maintenance needs, and the plant's size to ensure it thrives in your environment.

How do I change the pots?

To change a pot, gently remove the plant from its current pot, loosening the roots if necessary. Place the plant in a new pot slightly larger than the old one, fill with fresh soil, and water thoroughly.

How often should I water my plant?

Water your plant when the top inch of soil feels dry, but the frequency depends on the plant type, pot size, and environment. Ensure proper drainage to avoid waterlogging.
